CEL Trucks

For the past 30 years, CEL Trucks has been a trusted supplier of used road haulage and construction equipment for both the export and the domestic market. We have established and continue to uphold an outstanding reputation for delivering exactly what you need, where you need it, and when you need it.

Services

We offer prompt decisions on vehicles and machinery to purchase during your defleeting process, ensuring timely payments and collections. You can relax, knowing your vehicles and machinery have the livery removed before being sold to a new owner.

Additionally, we provide solutions for lease vehicle returns, helping you save money. We can match the buyout figure of your leased vehicles or fleet and accept them as-is, where-is, saving hauliers significant costs in returns to the leasing company.

We can arrange shipping to most major seaports worldwide, utilizing a variety of methods to suit your needs. Our services include containerization, CKD (Completely Knocked Down), and cost-effective piggyback loading and lashing. No matter your destination, we ensure efficient and reliable shipping.

For our UK-based customers, we offer timely domestic delivery of trucks and construction equipment.

We offer agency-structured services for resale, providing a secure compound with 24-hour monitored security to ensure the safety of your assets. Using our proven marketing strategies, we unlock the value in your redundant assets, maximizing their potential.

For our domestic customers, we offer competitive financing options with a quick turnaround.

Parts

We offer a wide range of high-quality operational parts. This environmentally friendly approach not only keeps trucks on the road but also diverts a significant amount of waste from landfills and processing facilities. We ensure the responsible recycling of all oils and metals, reducing our environmental footprint and contributing to a sustainable future."

Environment Policy

"We are deeply committed to environmental responsibility and recognize the significant impact of this industry on the environment. Taking this into serious consideration, we endeavour to mitigate our footprint. Our operations prioritize the use of modern vehicles equipped with clean engines, ensuring that our movements and deliveries are executed with maximum efficiency and minimal environmental impact. Combined with our ongoing policy on asset distribution and recycling, we are striving towards net zero."
